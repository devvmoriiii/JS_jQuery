NewYork

Info.

New York, often called New York City or NYC, is the most populous city in the United States. With a 2020 population of 8,804,190 distributed over 300.46 square miles (778.2 km2), New York City is the most densely populated major city in the United States.

Note

The city is more than twice as populous as Los Angeles, the nation's second-largest city. New York City is situated at the southern tip of New York State.
